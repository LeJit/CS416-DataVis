Speed Climbing Visualization

This data visualization will explore the performance of top male speed climbers. Speed climbing is a competitive climbing event where two climbers traverse a 15-meter wall as quickly as possible in a head-to-head competition.

Speed Climbing was added to the Tokyo Olympics in 2021 as part of the newly created rock-climbing sport. Speed Climbing will be featured in the Paris Olympics in 2024 as a category seperate from other rock-climbing disciplines, such as lead climbing and bouldering.
